## CalTrak

### Goal
The goal of this application is to help users track their current health goals, and stay in touch with those who can motivate
them to better reach their intended targets!

### Technologies Used:
* MongoDB
* AngularJS
* Express
* Node.js
* Bourbon/Neat (SASS)
* JSONWebTokens (Authorization)
